## 1. Purpose

This addendum defines additional handling expectations for MedData Nexus teams supporting public sector healthcare analytics contracts. It is a synthetic training document for Capstone 2 and does not represent a real government contract.

## 2. Data Handling Requirements

Public sector support records may include contract requirements, control evidence, service-level reports, and security questionnaire responses. These records are classified as Confidential unless they contain PHI, credentials, export-controlled information, or other Restricted-tier data.

## 3. AI Use Rules

- Internal RAG retrieval is permitted for read-only lookup of approved contract support templates and control narratives.
- External AI API submission requires documented authorization in the AI System Inventory.
- Restricted data may not be submitted to external AI APIs without CISO approval, Legal review, and documented data handling terms.
- AI-generated contract language must be reviewed by Legal Counsel before use.
- AI-generated security control statements must be reviewed by IT Security before submission to a government customer or assessor.

## 4. Evidence Requirements

For AI-assisted government contract support work, teams must retain:

- Source document references.
- Prompt or query text when used for substantive drafting.
- AI-generated draft output.
- Human reviewer name and approval date.
- Final submitted version.

## 5. Prohibited Uses

AI systems may not:

- Create unsupported compliance claims.
- Invent control evidence.
- Summarize Restricted data for external submission without approval.
- Replace Legal, Compliance, or Security sign-off.
