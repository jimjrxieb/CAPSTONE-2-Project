# AI Adoption Maturity Model

## Purpose

Use this model to rate MedData Nexus' AI adoption posture before recommending pilot, expansion, or remediation.

## Maturity Ratings

| Stage | Description | Evidence Required |
|---|---|---|
| Ad Hoc | AI usage exists through unsanctioned tools or personal accounts. | shadow AI indicators, no policy, no audit trail |
| Emerging | Approved pilots exist, but controls are incomplete. | pilot scope, basic policy, limited monitoring |
| Scaling | AI workflows cross teams and have defined controls. | governance, monitoring, review gates, validation tests |
| Mature | AI lifecycle is governed, monitored, tested, and improved continuously. | metrics, incident feedback, red-team cadence, executive reporting |

## Scale Recommendation

Use one:

- **Scale:** evidence supports controlled expansion.
- **Conditional scale:** expand only with listed remediation.
- **Pilot only:** continue limited use while controls mature.
- **Pause:** risk exceeds current control evidence.

## CBBP Interpretation

- Weak COMPLY means the client does not know what should happen.
- Weak BUILD means the client has policies but no enforceable controls.
- Weak BREAK means the client has claims but no validation.
- Weak PROVE means the client cannot defend the adoption decision.
